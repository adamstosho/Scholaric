# Scholaric - Executive Summary

## The Opportunity

**Scholaric** is a decentralized learn-to-earn platform that rewards students with cryptocurrency (cUSD) for correctly answering quiz questions. Built on the Celo blockchain with MiniPay integration, Scholaric addresses the $250+ billion EdTech market by making education accessible and financially rewarding.

## The Problem

Traditional education platforms suffer from:
- Lack of student motivation and engagement
- Limited global accessibility
- Opaque reward systems
- High transaction costs for micro-payments
- Geographic and payment barriers

## The Solution

Scholaric combines blockchain technology with education to create a transparent, accessible, and financially incentivized learning platform where:
- Students earn cUSD rewards for correct answers
- Educators create and monetize educational content
- All transactions are transparent and secure on-chain
- Global access with low-cost transactions via Celo

## Market Opportunity

- **Target Market**: $250+ billion EdTech market, growing to $350+ billion by 2025
- **Target Users**: 1.5+ billion students globally + 90+ million educators
- **Key Markets**: Africa (1.3B), Asia (4.7B), Latin America (650M)
- **Competitive Edge**: First learn-to-earn platform on Celo with native MiniPay integration

## Technology

- **Blockchain**: Celo Mainnet with verified smart contracts
- **Frontend**: Next.js 14, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Storage**: IPFS for decentralized content storage
- **Security**: Commit-reveal pattern, reentrancy protection, access controls
