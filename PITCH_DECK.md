## Slide 2: The Problem

### Current Education Challenges

**5 Critical Pain Points:**

1. **Lack of Motivation** 
   - Students lack financial incentives to engage with educational content
   - Low participation and completion rates

2. **Limited Accessibility**
   - Quality education is expensive and not accessible to everyone
   - Geographic and economic barriers prevent learning

3. **No Transparency**
   - Reward systems in traditional platforms are opaque
   - Students can't verify fairness or see how rewards are calculated

4. **High Transaction Costs**
   - Traditional payment systems charge high fees for small transactions
   - Micro-payments are economically unviable

5. **Geographic Barriers**
   - Students in developing regions face currency and payment barriers
   - Limited access to global educational opportunities

## Slide 5: How It Works

### Platform Flow

**For Students/Learners:**
1. Connect wallet (MiniPay, MetaMask, WalletConnect)
2. Browse quiz catalogue with filters
3. Join active quizzes
4. Answer questions using commit-reveal pattern
5. Earn cUSD rewards based on performance
6. View leaderboards and track earnings

**For Creators/Educators:**
1. Create quizzes with multiple question types
2. Upload content to IPFS (decentralized storage)
3. Fund prize pools with cUSD
4. Manage quiz parameters (duration, participants)
5. Track analytics and engagement

**For Everyone:**
- Transparent, on-chain reward distribution
- Fair, automated scoring system
- Secure, tamper-proof quiz data

## Appendix: Technical Details

### Smart Contract Information

**Contract: QuizManager**
- Network: Celo Mainnet
- Address: `0x1469beF9638eE24Bdb39835fD3429D45F7833827`
- Explorer: [Celoscan](https://celoscan.io/address/0x1469beF9638eE24Bdb39835fD3429D45F7833827)

**Key Functions:**
- `createQuiz()` - Create new quizzes
- `fundQuiz()` - Add funds to prize pools
- `joinQuiz()` - Participate in quizzes
- `commitAnswer()` - Submit answer commitments
- `revealAnswer()` - Reveal and score answers
- `distributeRewards()` - Automatic reward distribution

**Security Features:**
- Commit-reveal pattern for answer security
- Reentrancy protection
- Access control (Ownable)
- Pausable for emergencies
- OpenZeppelin security standards
