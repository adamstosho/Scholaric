   - Community building

## Presentation Tips

1. **Keep it Simple**: Avoid jargon, use plain language
2. **Tell Stories**: Use real-world examples
3. **Show, Don't Tell**: Use visuals and demos
4. **Be Enthusiastic**: Show passion for the problem
5. **Practice Timing**: Aim for 10-15 minutes, leave time for Q&A
6. **Prepare Backup**: Have videos ready if live demo fails
7. **Know Your Numbers**: Be ready to defend projections
8. **End Strong**: Clear call to action

---

## Slide Design Guidelines

- **Colors**: Use Celo green/yellow or your brand colors
- **Typography**: Clean, readable fonts (Poppins, Inter, etc.)
- **Imagery**: High-quality screenshots, icons, diagrams
- **Consistency**: Same style throughout
- **White Space**: Don't overcrowd slides
- **Contrast**: Ensure text is readable
